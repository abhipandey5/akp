About Me

Abhishek Kumar Pandey is a dedicated cybersecurity researcher, currently pursuing his PhD in Computer Science and Engineering at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad. With a stellar academic record and a deep passion for securing digital ecosystems, he is actively contributing to cutting-edge research at the Center for Security, Theory, and Algorithmic Research (C-STAR). Previously, he served as an Assistant Professor (Guest) at M.L.K. P.G. College, Balrampur and has been a key researcher in an advanced IT laboratory driving groundbreaking innovations in cybersecurity, particularly in healthcare data security.

With a research portfolio spanning 20+ published and internationally funded articles, his contributions in healthcare data security and management have been widely recognized and endorsed by leading researchers and scientists worldwide. During his academic journey, he consistently ranked among the most prolific contributors to prestigious international journals. Currently, his research focuses on machine learning-driven authentication systems and their applications in healthcare security.

Bringing over 4+ years of research and 2+ years of teaching experience, Abhishek is an active member of renowned scientific communities, including the London Journals Press, Internet Society India, and the International Association of Engineers.
